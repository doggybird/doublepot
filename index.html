Double Pot

3-Card Poker × Baccarat  •  two pots, no splits

Set the table

Ante: everyone posts 1 chip. The pot is cut in two.
Poker half: best 3-card hand — high card < pair < flush < straight < trips < straight flush < royal.
Baccarat half: sum of the 3 cards, closest to 9 (A=1, 10/J/Q/K=0, mod 10).
No chops: exact ties go to the higher cards (rank, then ♠>♥>♦>♣).
